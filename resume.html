At Michigan State University, I worked in the Digital Scholarship Lab at the Library. Here I assisted students in a space where they had access to a wealth of technology and programs that were not available anywhere else on campus, including but not limited to: Cad, Google SketchUp, Adobe CC, VR and 360 video technologies, and more.
My main project at this job was performing user tests on existing library technology and tutorials and making suggestions to improve usability and accessibility. I also Developed a style guide, documentation for increased consistency of outgoing communication from the MSU DSL.
Also at MSU, I worked for the Thesis and Dissertation office to edit Masters theses and doctoral dissertations for errors and formatting consistency.
